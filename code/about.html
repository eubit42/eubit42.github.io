Our Motto:

"Ex Cinere, Nos Resurgemus", or "Out of the ashes, We shall rise".

 Which means we, as an individual or society, will emerge as new from something that has been destroyed. This expression alludes to the legendary phoenix, which is a bird that supposedly rose from the ashes of its funeral pyre with renewed youth.
